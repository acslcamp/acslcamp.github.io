Meet the Team

Learn from experienced students who have succeeded at the highest levels of competitive math and coding.

Louis Junghyo Yoo

Co-Founder

Louis Junghyo Yoo is a rising sophomore at Lake Washington High School, and stands out as a student leader with a strong focus on mathematics and computer science. He has succeeded in several STEM competitions, including the highest AMC 10 score among freshmen and sophomores at Lake Washington High School, the KSEA competition series, USACO, and international events with his robotics team, winning several awards.

Anish Agrawal

Head of Instruction

Anish Agrawal engineers software across multiple robotics teams, building autonomous systems, custom odometry localization, and kinematic pathfinding engines. He has qualified for the USA Junior Mathematical Olympiad and USACO Gold, alongside delivering high-level performances in robotics tournaments. Passionate about tackling complex coding and math challenges, he continually works through personal projects to advance his thinking. Beyond his own work, Anish gives back to STEM education as a competitive math tutor and problem writer.

John Choe

Co-Founder

John Choe is a rising junior at Bellevue High School with a strong interest in competitive mathematics and coding. He has actively participated in numerous academic competitions, earning 6th place individually and 1st place as a team at the Math is Cool Masters competition, alongside another 1st place team finish at the Purple Comet math meet. Additionally, John has been involved with the KSEA competition series as well as prestigious national and international events, including USACO and AIME.

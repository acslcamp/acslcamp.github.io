Camp Details

Everything you need to know about the ACSL Prep Camp.

Schedule & Logistics

Our program will run fully online from August 3rd to 13th or August 17th to 27th, meeting Monday through Thursday from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM.

  • Tuition: Enrollment is $25 per student to cover the cost of team registration.
  • Materials: Digital textbooks and all necessary study resources are provided completely free of charge.
  • Location: Fully online via video conference.

Curriculum

Designed specifically for rising 4th to 8th graders, students will master all topics covered in the ACSL Junior Division, learning directly from experienced student coders.

  • Python Syntax: Everything required to compete in the programming portion of the ACSL exams.
  • Number Systems: Base 2, 8, and 16 conversions and arithmetic.
  • Prefix/Infix/Postfix Notation: Evaluating and translating expressions.
  • Boolean Algebra: Logic gates and boolean expressions.
  • Graph Theory & Bit-String Flicking: Essential concepts for the written exams.
  • What Does This Program Do? (WDTPD): Tracing pseudocode to determine outputs.
  • Digital Electronics: Understanding logic circuits and their Boolean representations.
  • LISP Programming: Functional programming concepts and basic list manipulation.

Official ACSL Exam Opportunity

Participants will gain the unique opportunity to take the official ACSL exam during the 2026–2027 season. More camps will take place before the official exam.
